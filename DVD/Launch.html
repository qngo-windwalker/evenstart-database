Even Start Family Literacy Program Database

This database brings together research and practical information on family literacy and family literacy program implementation. It was originally collected for the federal Even Start program, but it holds implications for anyone working with integrated family education. It can also be of use to those working within any of the four component areas of the Even Start platform: early childhood education, adult education, parenting education, and interactive literacy activities. These materials have been logged and compiled into a searchable database for your convenience. The hope is that these materials continue to further the goals of family literacy programs and other services that seek to educate most-in-need families and narrow the achievement gap.
